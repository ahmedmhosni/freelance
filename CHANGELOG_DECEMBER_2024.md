#### Email Notification Preferences
Take control of your inbox! You can now customize which emails you receive from the platform.

**What you can do:**
- **Marketing Emails** - Control promotional content, tips, and special offers
- **Notification Emails** - Manage task reminders, invoice updates, and activity alerts
- **Platform Updates** - Choose whether to receive new feature announcements

**How to access:**
1. Go to your Profile page
2. Click the "Email Preferences" tab
3. Toggle each preference on or off
4. Changes save automatically

**Note:** Important security and account-related emails will always be sent regardless of your preferences.

---

#### Data Privacy & GDPR Compliance
We respect your data rights. New privacy controls are now available in your profile.

**Export Your Data:**
- Request a complete copy of all your data
- Includes clients, projects, tasks, invoices, and time entries
- Receive a download link via email within 15-30 minutes
- Rate limited to once per 24 hours for security

**Delete Your Account:**
- Permanently delete your account and all associated data
- Requires password confirmation for security
- Optional: Tell us why you're leaving to help us improve
- Action cannot be undone - we recommend exporting your data first

**How to access:**
1. Go to your Profile page
2. Click the "Data & Privacy" tab
3. Choose "Request Data Export" or "Delete Account"

**Compliance:** Fully compliant with GDPR Articles 15 (Right of Access), 17 (Right to Erasure), and 20 (Data Portability)
